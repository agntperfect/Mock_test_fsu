Examination Notice:

  • The duration of this exam is 2 hr. It has 12 questions — the first 8 carry 1 mark each and the remaining 4 carry 2 marks each, for a total of 16 marks.
  • You have to select the radio button on the correct option for each question.
  • You can change your option any time during the examination period.
  • To leave a question blank, simply don't select any option. The list of unanswered questions can be viewed by clicking “View unanswered” on your page.
  • Information along with time remaining, total attempted and total questions will be displayed in the side panel during the exam.
  • When the time is up, your exam will be submitted automatically.
  • Your progress is saved on this device, so an accidental refresh won't lose your answers. (sample copy — this standalone preview doesn't actually persist anything)
