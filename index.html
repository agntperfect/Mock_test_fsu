Examination Notice:

  • The duration of this exam is 2 hr. It has 12 questions across 4 subjects, for a total of 16 marks.
  • Each question shows its own mark value beside it. A wrong answer deducts 25% of that question's marks; leaving a question blank scores 0 with no penalty.
  • You have to select the radio button on the correct option for each question. You can change your option any time during the examination period.
  • The list of unanswered questions can be viewed by clicking “View unanswered” on your page.
  • Information along with time remaining, total attempted and total questions will be displayed in the side panel during the exam.
  • When the time is up, your exam will be submitted automatically.
  • After submitting, you can review your answers against the correct ones and see a subject-wise breakdown, right here in this preview.
  • Your progress is saved on this device, so an accidental refresh won't lose your answers.
